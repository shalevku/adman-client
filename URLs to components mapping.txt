URLs to Components mappings:
/ads -> AdsTable, /ads?component=Carousel -> Carousel
/ads/:id -> existingAdForm
/ads/:id?component=Deleted -> Div (ad deleted)
/users -> UsersTable
/users/:id -> existingUserForm, /loginUserForm -> loginUserForm, /newUserForm -> newUserForm
/users/:id?component=Deleted -> Div (user deleted)
